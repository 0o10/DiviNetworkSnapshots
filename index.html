How To Update Your Chain

OSX Manual Snapshot

DIVI-Snapshot.zip

Part 1

Download the snapshot file called DIVI-Snapshot.zip and double-click. This action will unzip the file and show a folder named DIVI-Snapshot.
  1. Inside this folder, you will find 2 subfolders.
    • blocks
    • chainstate
Remember the location of these four folders we will need them in Part 3, Step 1

Part 2

Close Divi Desktop Application (exit, do not minimize, close completely) Open Finder Click GO at the top of Finder Select the "Go to Folder" option in the drop-down list In the "Go to Folder" Search Bar enter ~/Library/Application Support/ (pay close attention to the placement of the tilda ~ ) Click the blue Go button Open folder titled "DIVI" (not Divi Desktop) Keep / Preserve in place the following four items. backups(directory) – Do Not Move debug.log masternode.conf – Do Not Move wallet.dat – Do Not Move Optional You may or may not have any other wallets with wallet_#######.dat. If you do move them to the backups(directory) above but make sure to keep the file titled "wallet.dat" preserved and in place. Clean "DIVI" folder by sending all other files, except the above to trash When done all that will be in the folder titled "DIVI" will be backups(directory) – Do Not Move debug.log masternode.conf – Do Not Move wallet.dat – Do Not Move

Part 3

Open your DIVI-Snapshot folder from Part 1, Step 1 Move or Copy (2 Folders) blocks chainstate To the "DIVI" Folder you just cleaned above (Part 2, Step 8) Close Finder Window

Part 4

Restart Divi Desktop Application If "Primer" pops up click "Cancel" button Your Divi Desktop Application will fully sync soon after!
